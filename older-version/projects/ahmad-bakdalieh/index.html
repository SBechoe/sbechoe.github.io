About Me


Aspiring full-stack developer with practical experience. In general, I had an international childhood: I was born and raised in Damascus, Syria, and I moved to the Netherlands when I was 14.

I am currently studying Software Development at ROC Mondriaan in The Hague. I am a hard-working, self-motivated, and a fast learner. I am also a team player and I am always open to new opportunities.

Due to the situation in my country, I received part of my education in Egypt and Turkey. I first came to the Netherlands in 2017 and started learning Dutch. Meanwhile, I enjoy building websites and solving problems to get to a satisfactory result for the client.

My practical knowledge consists of algorithms, servers, Object Oriented Programming and security by design principles. My ambition is to study more about computer science and perhaps study abroad in the future.

For my personal projects I build and maintain web pages using the Bootstrap, Vue and ExpressJS frameworks. As a hobby I like to skate now and then and like to play video games and listen to music.

I am a creative person and very good at solving problems when it comes to algorithms and making software that is secure by design.
